Welcome to the EscapeCharacters Project Website

We are currently working on the android application dubbed, 'What's Happenin', in the Software Engineering course (CS330), over the course of the Fall 2012 semester.  This mobile application is envisioned to be a social media hub of the CSB/SJU and St. Joe community.  Looking for something to do on a Friday night at SJU?  Want to know 'what's happenin' in St. Joe?  What's 'hoppin' at CSB?  Then this is the app for you.  It will bring together the big hit attractions of these three communities into one location, and tell you what's 'hot' and what's 'not'.

Aside from providing an easy method available at the tips of your fingers to find a way to make your boring life more interesting, you can also rate, comment, and check-in to different locations and events on campus or in town.  Let everyone else know what is going on, what to hit up, and what to avoid.  Not only can you see what is happening around the CSB/SJU community, but you can also add to everyone's experience by introducing your own parties, events, and happenin's!
